Weight Gain

Veg Diet & Nutrition

Early morning

  • 1 glass of cucumber-infused water on an empty stomach

Breakfast

Choose any meal below as per your choice :

  • A bowl of oats porridge preferably with skimmed milk and mixed nuts of your choice.
  • 2 wheat bread stuffed with mixed vegetables along with 1 cup of yogurt.
  • 2 slices of multigrain toast/bowl of peas along with a cup of skimmed milk/yogurt.
  • A smoothie with nuts, fruit, and yogurt along with any fruit of your choice.
  • 2 idlis with a bowl of sambar.
  • You can have two besan chilla and green garlic chutney.

Lunch

Choose any meal below as per your choice :

  • 2 wheat bread with lentils and mixed vegetable curry.
  • Half a bowl of brown rice along with a vegetable lentil curry of choice.
  • A bowl of vegetables along with paneer curry and green chutney.
  • Eat one vegetable or leafy green stuffed wheat bread with
    low-fat paneer curry and dal or A bowl of steamed brown
    rice and spinach for lunch.

Dinner

Choose any meal below as per your choice :

  • 2 wheat bread with lentils or vegetable curry or steamed leafy vegetables.
  • Half a bowl of leafy greens with rice and 1 cup of lentil curry of your choice.
  • Half a bowl of steamed brown rice and 1 cup of Chickpea curry.
  • A cup of lentils with one wheat bread and little vegetables.
  • Low-fat paneer curry and one vegetable or leafy green stuffed wheat bread.

Fillers

  • In between fillers (if hungry) for all seven days include skimmed milk paneer (100 gm), one bowl of salad, 1 cup of seasonal fruits, and a glass of buttermilk, or a cup of tea with less milk and jaggery.

Non-Veg Diet & Nutrition

Early morning

  • 1 glass of cucumber-infused water on an empty stomach

Breakfast

Choose any meal below as per your choice :

  • Bowl of sambar with two brown rice idlis.
  • Low-fat paneer sandwich with mint chutney.
  • 2 chana dal pancakes stuffed with mixed vegetables and a glass of milk/bread.
  • One boiled egg with a bowl of seasonal fruits or Egg omelette (made from 2 eggs).
  • Vegetable dal and a glass of milk.
  • 3-4 idlis with sambar.

Lunch

Choose any meal below as per your choice :

  • 2 whole-grain bread with a bowl of mixed-vegetable curry and one cup of dal.
  • A bowl of fish curry with brown rice.
  • 2 whole-grain bread with a bowl of non-veg curry of your choice.
  • Large bowl of salad with rajma curry and brown rice
  • One bowl millet and dal khichdi with multigrain bread.

Dinner

Choose any meal below as per your choice :

  • Chicken curry with mixed vegetables and a fresh spinach salad.
  • Chicken gravy with one multigrain bread.
  • A bowl of khichdi with sprout salad.
  • Chickpea curry with basmati rice and green salad.
  • Lentil pancakes with chicken tikka masala.
  • Chicken curry with 80-100gm boiled sweet potatoes and
    mixed vegetables/chicken curry with one multigrain bread.

Fillers

  • In between fillers (if hungry) for all seven days include skimmed milk paneer (100 gm), one bowl of salad, 1 cup of seasonal fruits, and a glass of buttermilk, or a cup of tea with less milk and jaggery.
